IT Power per CDU
每台 CDU 的 IT 功率
Total IT equipment power load handled by one CDU unit. For high-density GPU racks (e.g. NVIDIA H100), this can exceed 100 kW per rack.
每台 CDU 處理的 IT 設備總功率。高密度 GPU 機架（如 NVIDIA H100）每架可超過 100 kW。
Q = IT Power × PUE
✔ Typical range: 50–500 kW
kW
?
Number of CDU Units
CDU 台數
Total number of Cooling Distribution Units in the system. Each CDU serves a set of server racks via a liquid cooling loop.
系統中的冷卻分配單元總台數。每台 CDU 透過液冷迴路服務一組伺服器機架。
Total Heat = IT Power × CDU Count × PUE
✔ Typical: 1–20 units per zone
?
PUE — Power Usage Effectiveness
PUE — 電源使用效率
Ratio of total facility power to IT equipment power. PUE = 1.0 is ideal (100% efficient). Liquid cooling achieves PUE 1.03–1.15 vs air cooling 1.4–2.0.
總設施功率與 IT 設備功率的比值。PUE = 1.0 為理想值。液冷可達 1.03–1.15，遠優於風冷的 1.4–2.0。
PUE = Total Facility Power / IT Power
✔ Liquid cooling target: 1.03–1.15
Typical: 1.05–1.15 for liquid cooling 液冷典型值：1.05–1.15

Supply Water Temperature (Tw,in)
供水溫度 (Tw,in)
Inlet water temperature to the CDU from the facility chilled water system. Lower supply temp increases cooling capacity but raises chiller energy cost.
CDU 從設施冰水系統接收的進水溫度。供水溫度越低冷卻能力越強，但冷機能耗越高。
ΔT = Tw,out − Tw,in
✔ ASHRAE W1: 20°C | W2: 25°C | W3: 35°C
°C
?
Return Water Temperature (Tw,out)
回水溫度 (Tw,out)
Outlet water temperature leaving the CDU back to the facility. Higher return temp means better energy efficiency. ΔT = Tw,out − Tw,in drives flow calculation.
從 CDU 回到設施的出水溫度。回水溫度越高代表能源效率越好。ΔT = 回水 − 供水，是流量計算的關鍵。
ṁ = Q / (Cp × ΔT)
✔ Typical ΔT: 10–20°C
°C
?
Coolant Type
冷卻液類型
Pure water has best heat capacity (Cp = 4.182 kJ/kg·K). Glycol mixtures reduce freeze risk but lower Cp, requiring higher flow rates for same cooling.
純水比熱容最佳 (Cp = 4.182 kJ/kg·K)。乙二醇混合液防凍但 Cp 較低，相同冷卻量需要更大流量。
Q = ṁ × Cp × ΔT
✔ Data center: usually pure water or ≤25% glycol
Facility Side設施側參數

Chilled Water Supply Temp (Facility side)
冰水供應溫度（設施側）
Chilled water temperature supplied from the chiller plant to the CDU. Typical data center chiller supply: 7–15°C. Higher temp allows free cooling.
冷水機組提供給 CDU 的冰水溫度。資料中心冷機典型供水：7–15°C。溫度越高越有機會使用自由冷卻。
✔ Typical: 7–15°C | Free cooling: 18–24°C
°C
?
Chilled Water Return Temp (Facility side)
冰水回水溫度（設施側）
Return temperature of chilled water back to the chiller. ΔT = CHW,out − CHW,in determines facility-side flow rate. Larger ΔT = smaller pipe & pump size.
冰水回到冷機的溫度。ΔT = 回水 − 供水，決定設施側流量。ΔT 越大，管道與水泵尺寸越小。
CHW Flow = Q / (Cp × ΔT_chw)
✔ Typical ΔT: 5–8°C
°C
📐 Engineering Formulas 工程計算公式
Q = ṁ × Cp × ΔT
Heat Load = Mass Flow × Specific Heat × ΔT 熱負載 = 質量流量 × 比熱容 × 溫差
ṁ = Q / (Cp × ΔT)
Mass Flow [kg/s] from known Q & ΔT 由已知 Q 與 ΔT 求質量流量 [kg/s]
V̇ = ṁ / ρ
Volume Flow [m³/h] from mass flow & density 由質量流量與密度求體積流量 [m³/h]

🖥️ Rack Heat Load Calculator 機架熱負載計算

Number of Racks
機架數量
Total server racks in the data hall. Used to calculate total IT load and required number of CDUs. Modern hyperscale DC: 1,000–10,000+ racks.
資料機房的伺服器機架總數。用於計算 IT 總負載與所需 CDU 台數。現代超大規模 DC：1,000–10,000+ 架。
Total Load = Racks × Power/Rack
✔ Typical hall: 100–500 racks
?
Power per Rack
每架功率
Average IT power load per rack. Traditional air-cooled: 5–15 kW. High-density GPU racks: 30–100+ kW. NVIDIA GB200 NVL72: up to 120 kW/rack.
每架平均 IT 功率負載。傳統風冷：5–15 kW。高密度 GPU 機架：30–100+ kW。NVIDIA GB200 NVL72：最高 120 kW/架。
✔ AI/GPU rack: 30–120 kW | Standard: 5–15 kW
kW / rack
?
Liquid Cooling Coverage (%)
液冷覆蓋率 (%)
Percentage of rack heat removed by liquid cooling. The remainder is handled by air cooling (CRAC/CRAH). DLC/immersion: 70–100%. RDHX: 50–70%.
液冷移除熱量的百分比，其餘由風冷（CRAC/CRAH）處理。直接液冷/浸沒：70–100%。後門熱交換器：50–70%。
✔ DLC/Immersion: 70–100% | RDHX: 50–70%
% of heat removed by liquid (rest by air)% 由液冷移除的熱量 (其餘由風冷)
?
Supply Temp to Rack
機架供水溫度
Water temperature supplied to each server rack CDU manifold. Must be below server thermal limits. ASHRAE W1 spec: ≤20°C for standard operation.
供應給每架伺服器 CDU 歧管的水溫。必須低於伺服器熱限制。ASHRAE W1 標準：標準運行 ≤20°C。
✔ ASHRAE W1: ≤20°C | W2: ≤25°C
°C

Return Temp from Rack
機架回水溫度
Water temperature returning from server rack. High return temp = good energy recovery potential. Direct-to-chip liquid cooling typically returns 40–55°C.
從伺服器機架回水的溫度。回水溫越高代表廢熱回收潛力越好。晶片直冷液冷典型回水：40–55°C。
ΔT = Tout − Tin
✔ D2C typical: 40–55°C
