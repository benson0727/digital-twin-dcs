Under the hood

Three domains, one live model.

Heat Rejection & ΔT Tracking

Models supply/return ΔT across the CDU heat exchanger and flags drift before it becomes a rack-level thermal event.

  • Liquid-to-liquid and liquid-to-air CDU configurations
  • Rack inlet/outlet temperature projection
  • Approach temperature and fouling trend tracking
> supply_temp: 27.4°C  target: 27.0°C
> return_temp: 35.8°C
> delta_t: 8.4K  status: NOMINAL
> approach_temp: 2.1K

Flow Distribution & Pressure Drop

Tracks flow rate against pump curve and system pressure drop, so valve and pump sizing stay matched to actual load.

  • Variable-speed pump response modeling
  • Valve authority and Cv sizing checks
  • Pressure drop across BPHE / PHE under load
> flow_rate: 142 L/min
> pressure_drop: 1.8 bar
> pump_speed: 68%
> valve_position: 74% OPEN

Thresholds & Alerts

Sets operating bands per rack or per CDU, and surfaces the reading the moment it moves outside spec.

  • Configurable high/low thresholds per sensor
  • Trend view for shift handover
  • Exportable log for review meetings
> alert_high: 32.0°C
> alert_low: 18.0°C
> current: 27.4°C  status: OK
> last_breach: none in 30d
